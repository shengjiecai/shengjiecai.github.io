This is a sample reading note. Replace this file with your own reading notes.

## Bibliographic information

- Author: Author Name
- Title: Book or paper title
- Year: 2026
- Topic: Macroeconomics, political economy, or another field

## Main question

What is the central question of the reading? State it in one or two sentences.

## Core argument

Summarize the mechanism or argument. For academic readings, it is useful to distinguish the research question, theoretical mechanism, empirical strategy, identification assumptions, and contribution to the literature.

## My comments

Write your own evaluation here. For example:

- What is persuasive?
- What is weak or incomplete?
- How does this connect to my own research?

> A short quotation or personal reflection can be placed here.
